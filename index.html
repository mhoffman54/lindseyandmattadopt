A little about us...

We’re Lindsey and Matt. We live in a quiet neighborhood in the Minneapolis suburbs, where the streets are lined with trees and the neighbors actually know each other's names.

Our life isn't perfect, but it's full. It's full of love and laughter, the smell of fresh pancakes on Saturday mornings, and the constant pitter-patter of paws on the floor.

Meet Lindsey

I'm the one who remembers every birthday and makes sure we always have the good snacks. I've always wanted to be a mom—to read the bedtime stories, to dry the tears, and to cheer the loudest at every event.

Meet Matt

I'm the guy who’s usually in the garage or the theater room working on a project. I'm ready to teach a kid how to build things, how to be kind, and how to never give up—even when the Vikings are losing.
